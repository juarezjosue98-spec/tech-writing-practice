# How to Reset Your Password Using the Company IT Portal
## Purpose 
This procedure ensures that employees can securely regain access to their company account.
## Scope
Onboarding employees or any employee who is unable to sign-in to the company account or is required to submit a password change due to password expiry.
## Prerequisites
- PC or MAC: A company-provided PC or MAC is available for guest use to reset account passwords. Contact IT if you do not have access to a company device.
- Company Email: Required access to the company-associated email is necessary to receive the password reset link. If unable to access email, contact the IT support team.
## Numbered Procedure Steps
1. Open your browser and navigate to `itportal.company.com`.
2. Click **Log In**.
3. Click **Forgot Password**
4. Enter your email address to receive the reset link.
5. Check your email inbox for the provided reset password message.   
   *If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, 
   check your spam folder.*
    > ⚠️ **Warning:** Do not reuse a previous password 
    > or use an easily guessed password. Weak passwords 
    > may result in account suspension.
6. Create a new password that meets the company's 
   security requirements.
7. Re-enter your new password in the 
   **Confirm Password** field.
8. Click **Save** or **Submit** to apply the 
   password change.
9. Check your email inbox for a confirmation message 
   indicating your password was successfully updated.
10. Return to `itportal.company.com` and click 
    **Log In** to access your account with the 
    new password.
